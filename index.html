The creator of NFT will have 97.5% (2.5% transaction fee) from the first sale of his NFTs and also 5% from each subsequent resale of his NFT on our in-game marketplace.

Thus, we create an opportunity not only to play with those weapons and characters that you yourself want, but also to make money on the creation of your own piece of art.

Players can earn IGT by fighting on arena battles, winning tournaments, creating NFTs, selling equipment on in-game marketplace and many more.

Moreover, IGT is used as a governance token of iDos Games Metaverse. Our community can participate in a decentralized decision-making process regarding metaverse and its projects development using their IGTs to vote.
